Evil Spirit

The Evil Spirit is a minor antagonist of the Cartoon Network miniseries Over the Garden Wall, serving as the main antagonist of the episode "The Ringing of the Bell". It is a man-eating spirit that possesses Lorna, forcing Auntie Whispers to keep it in check via a bell. Since it spent most of its screentime possessing Lorna, it was voiced by Shannyn Sossamon.

Adelaide

Adelaide, also known as Adelaide of the Pasture, is the secondary antagonist of the Cartoon Network miniseries Over the Garden Wall. She is Auntie Whispers' sister and a servant of the Beast, symbolizing false hope. She was voiced by John Cleese, who also portrayed the High Priest in Life of Brian and the French Taunter in Monty Python and the Holy Grail and even voiced Cat R. Waul in An American Tail: Fievel Goes West.

The Beast

The Beast is the main antagonist of the Cartoon Network miniseries Over the Garden Wall. He is the enigmatic monster of the place known as the Unknown. His goal is to take the souls of the people who come to his woods—in this case, the brothers Wirt and Greg.

Wirt

Wirt is the older half-brother of Gregory. He is the protagonist of the show, and views the world in a serious way, unlike his brother. He is determined and often makes a point of being a contrarian, which frequently leads the pair into trouble. He is voiced by Elijah Wood.

wiki fandom

Gregory

Gregory, affectionately nicknamed Greg, is Wirt's younger half-brother. He is a very energetic boy who always has a smile on his face and very wild ideas on his mind. He's endlessly fascinated by everything, including things that would normally seem scary such as the ghost in Mad Love. He has a habit of wandering out on his own. He has a pet frog, which he found before his descent into the Unknown. He gives it a different name every chapter (Kitty, Doctor, George Washington, etc) but ends up naming it Jason Funderburker in Chapter 10. He also carries a rock (that was stolen from Mrs. Daniel's garden), that will randomly make Rock Facts. He is the deuteragonist of the show.

Beatrice

Beatrice is the tritagonist and the false antagonist of the series, she is a talking bluebird that accompanies Wirt and Gregory, giving little lines of advice to them every now and then. and is described by her voice actor to be somewhat of a grumpy bird. Beatrice is a daughter among many other children (presumably 10), who previously lived in the Old Grist Mill where the Woodsman ground Edelwood.

Woodsman

The Woodsman is a character who appears in Over the Garden Wall. The Woodsman can potentially be seen as an ex-follower of the Beast. However, the Woodsman only chopped Edelwood trees as the Beast told him to so that his daughter's soul would stay lit in the Dark Lantern. This did not mean that he was a villain, often helping the two boys. The Woodsman is voiced by Christopher Lloyd, best known for his role as Doc Brown in the Back to the Future films and the Necromancer Rasputin in Don Bluth's Anastasia (1997).

Jason Funderburker

Gregory's pet frog is a character who appears in Over the Garden Wall. Throughout the story, the Frog appears to be a narrator of the brother's journey through the Dark Forest. He has many unnatural abilities that include the ability to talk (as well as sing) and play piano.

Sara

Sara is a character who appears in Episode 9 and 10, "My Reverie | Into the Unknown Part 1-2” (voiced by Emily Brundige). She makes two appearances - in the flashback through the two explodes. She is the school mascot (the bee) of Wirt's high school.
